A proposol for the future
CET is one of Kerala's top Engineering colleges with over 4000 students from across the country.
Yet it seriously lacks what many other colleges, without such a rich legacy, continue to boast about, an air conditioned hall able to accomodate a large number of students.
Sure, CGPU hall and CETAA hall are AC halls, but they have limited space and aren't suitable for conducting any cultural programmes. Even during the time of admission, students are seen sweltering in the heat on literally their first day on the college campus.
This needs to change, and this renovation aims to do exactly that.
What we need
01
A fully air conditioned hall
Temperatures are up throughout the majority of the academic year, this means that most of the programmes are hard to conduct during daytime indoors, without AC or proper cooling.
02
Replacement and upgradation of seats
Broken seats are literred across the hall, and it's hard for even the hall managers to tell which seat is broken and which is not. It is a matter of great shame for all college associatives if a guest sits on a broken chair by mistake
03
Upgradation of stage lights and equipments
It is also essential to have better lighting on stage, especially for cultural events. Moreover stage equipment including but not limited to:
  • Screen behind stage
  • Sound equipment such as mics and speakers
  • Extended cables for display support (to plug in laptop to screen)
  • And much more
    • 04
    Better interior design
    Instead of generic white walls with nothing on them, the hall can be decorated with art pieces depicting historically relevant CET related events, or be used to represent the various departments of the college. For this, the exclusive help of architecture department can be taken, while also utilizing creativity of the young minds at the college.
    05
    Backstage area
    A fully-flegged backstage is essential for properly conducting various cultural and even technical events. The backstage area should be equipped with mirrors and other equipment of the like to ensure maximum use.
    Why support us
    You might be wondering why we would consider you to donate. How is this going to benefit you? After all you have already passed out and are not likely to gain much use out of a renovated hall in your college.
    Wrong. By supporting the renovation costs you're not only helping the upcoming generation of students, you're increasing the value of the college you studied in. No longer will students within Kerala look to IITs or NITs for top tier programmes, they will embrace CET's new hall with renewed enthusiasm and love.
    Moreover, students outside the state from all over India will show a much higher involvment in CET's programmes, which will directly or indirectly affect the reputation of the college you studied in positively.
    So what are you waiting for?
